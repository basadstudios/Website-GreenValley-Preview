Local Craft Workshop

Learn traditional crafts from local artisans in this hands-on workshop. All materials are provided, and no prior experience is necessary.

This workshop is part of our "Preserving Heritage Skills" program, which aims to keep traditional crafting techniques alive in our community. Each workshop focuses on a different craft that has historical significance to our village.

What to Expect:

  • Professional instruction from master craftspeople
  • All necessary materials and tools included
  • Small group setting (maximum 12 participants)
  • Take home your completed craft project
  • Refreshments and snacks provided

This month's focus will be on traditional basket weaving, a craft that has been practiced in our village for generations. You'll learn about the history of basket weaving in our region and create your own small basket to take home.

Spaces are limited to ensure personalized instruction, so early registration is recommended.
